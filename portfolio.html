Disease Modelling: COVID-19 in France

Developed a SVIR compartmental model to simulate the spread of COVID-19 in France (June–September 2021). Conducted data fitting using MATLAB and built exponential growth/decay models, calculating key metrics like doubling time and half-life. Sensitivity analysis and parameter optimization were also performed.

Tools: MATLAB, ode45, fminsearch, epidemiological modeling
